Dog Lover

At the age of ten, my parents brought home my first golden retriever “Axi”. We grew up together and he became my best friend and one of the closest family members. He died from cancer at the age of thirteen and broke my heart. Dogs are not my whole life, but they do make my life a whole. Now I am living with my 2-year-old English cream golden retriever “Eggy”. Every time I look at him, I feel I am blessed. He motivates me to affectionate, seek adventure, and be loyal.

Xiaorui Zhang

Get busy living, or get busy dying.
